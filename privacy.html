Privacy Policy of Drink Responsibly!

Drink Responsibly! - the app to calculate and control BAC in your body.

This application only needs two types of your personal information - age and gender, this is necessary for the correct BAC calculation. All information you enter into the app is not transmitted from your device and is only stored locally on your device. Also, this app doesn't include any advertising or third-party analytics.

In case you have any questions or suggestions, please use drinkresponsiblyapp@gmail.com for contact.
